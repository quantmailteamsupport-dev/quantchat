## 3. Advanced Feature Breakdown

### A. E2EE & Network Security Resilience
Security is the absolute core of Nexus. We operate on a "Blind Backend" policy where user data is opaque to the servers.
- **WebSocket Pre-Key Rotation:** Implements the Signal Protocol’s Double Ratchet and X3DH algorithms. Communication keys are rotated seamlessly via secure WebSockets.
- **WebRTC Hardened Overlays:** Real-time peer-to-peer Audio and Video calls bypass insecure central servers. The architecture features jittered exponential backoffs and fast-fallback mechanisms ensuring absolute connection reliability across varying network conditions.

### B. Augmented Reality & 3D Immersion (Nexus Echo)
To combat Snapchat, Project Nexus utilizes advanced spatial computing elements.
- Uses WebXR for immersive, interactive 3D Holographic UI components overlaid during video calls.
- "Nexus Echo" focuses on E2EE ephemeral content sharing combined with localized AI AR filters.

### C. Web3 Auto-Monetization & Status Loops
Integrating behavioral psychology directly into tokenomics:
- **ReferralEngine & Status-as-a-Service:** A viral growth strategy grading users into Social Token Tiers (Spark, Connector, Influencer, Architect, Oracle).
- **Crypto Wallets:** Built-in Phantom/Solana wallet integrations for seamless micro-transactions unlocking premium features without traditional fiat banking constraints.

### D. Local On-Device AI & Moderation
- **TensorFlow.js Spam/Abuse Detection:** Moderation logic happens *on the client's device*, ensuring zero privacy breaches while efficiently removing malicious intent before hitting the broader network.
- **Memory-Optimized Models:** On-device AI acts continuously, adapting to latency and falling back safely when required by device hardware constraints.
